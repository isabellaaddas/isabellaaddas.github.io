NAPLES STYLE

Ingredients

This panuozzo-style sandwich is inspired by the classic Neapolitan recipe. Ingredients: homemade dough (baked daily), prosciutto, grated parmigiano reggiano, sliced provolone, arugula, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Allergen Information

Contains: gluten (in bread), pork, dairy. Substitutions can be made for gluten-free bread, meat type (or none), and no cheese. Cross-contamination may include contact with other gluten products, dairy products, meat products, nuts, eggs, soy, and fish.
