T & Z Deli

LOCATION

T & Z Deli is proudly the first-of-its-kind Italian-Korean fusion deli in New Jersey. Over 15 years of quality service has brought the deli to success, making it possible to expand the menu even further. This store is unique both in cuisine and atmosphere, run by a married couple and their eclectic family. T & Z Deli combines cultural food and values in a bold mix that customers will never forget. From porchetta and gelato to kimchi and seaweed soup, there's a little bit of everything for all palates. Check out the menu for the owners' tour of Italy and Korea, bringing a taste of both to the streets of Jersey.
